ABOUT

My name is Nechaev Vadim. I am from Rostov-on-Don, Russia. I am a student of the Institute of Mathematics, Mechanics and Computer Science. I have many different interests. I'm interested in computer science and constantly learning something new. But I want not only to study what is already there, but also to come up with something new, something that will be very useful and interesting for others. I also do dancing for myself. I am a member of the MMDANCE team, which appeared in its time on the faculty. Now this collective is not just the whole of the Southern Federal University, but the people's collective, which takes part in many competitions and competitions, some of which are of an all-Russian level. As a member of the main staff of the team, I often speak at various events. However, I speak not only in the staff, but also myself, for example on student's spring, the gubernatorial ball, etc. Sometimes it even seems to me that this is not just a hobby or one interest, but something more.
